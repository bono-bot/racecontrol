<objective>
Wave 1b (parallel with 26-02): Implement PIN-01 and PIN-02 — separate customer and staff
PIN failure counters in AppState, and counter logic in validate_pin() and
validate_employee_pin().

Purpose: Exhausting customer PIN attempts cannot lock out the staff debug PIN path.
Two separate HashMaps make the separation structural — a refactor cannot accidentally
merge them.

Output: AppState gains two new RwLock<HashMap<String, u32>> fields. validate_pin()
increments the customer counter on failure. validate_employee_pin() increments the staff
counter on failure. Customer lockout threshold is 5. Staff has NO lockout ceiling (PIN-02
is absolute: staff must always be able to unlock).
</objective>
