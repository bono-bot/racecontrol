<objective>
Wire EscalatingBackoff into AppState with pre-populated entries for all 8 pods, and harden rc-agent startup to fail fast with a branded lock screen error on invalid or missing config.

Purpose: Phase 2 (watchdog hardening) depends on pod_backoffs being pre-populated so pod_monitor and pod_healer share state without races. Config validation prevents pods from silently starting as "Pod 01" with wrong settings, which causes billing confusion and incorrect pod identification.

Output: Modified state.rs with pre-populated backoffs, modified main.rs with validation + no-default-fallback, modified lock_screen.rs with ConfigError variant, first-boot email test in rc-core startup.
</objective>
