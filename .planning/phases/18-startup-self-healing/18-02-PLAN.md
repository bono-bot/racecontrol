Add the StartupReport protocol message so rc-agent reports its startup status (version, uptime, config hash, crash recovery, repairs) to rc-core immediately after WebSocket connection, giving operators visibility into pod health on every boot.

Output: New `AgentMessage::StartupReport` in protocol.rs, sent from rc-agent main.rs, handled in rc-core ws/mod.rs.

       tracing::info!(
